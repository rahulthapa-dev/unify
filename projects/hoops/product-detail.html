Turnaround times

Average turnaround times are shown on the product page. Please note that these are average turnaround times only and are shown in working days which does not include weekends or public holidays. If you need your order for a specific date then check the express delivery options below.

Awesome free delivery

We are delighted to offer free delivery on all orders within the UK. We aim to dispatch all orders with free delivery within 8 working days and your package will be sent with Royal Mail or DPD depending on how much it weighs.

Please note: for offshore or remote UK destinations shipping costs may vary. Please check the couriered delivery exceptions below, and for further information just get in touch!

Fast 5 day delivery

Need your order within 5 working days? No problem! Just add Fast 5 Day Delivery* when you get to the checkout. We'll make sure to ship it out with DPD and you'll get tracking information to follow your delivery once it leaves our factory...

*Please make sure that when you add Fast 5 day delivery to your order you inform us of your deadline! You can do this in the comment box when you place your order, via email to info@my.hoopscrm.com or simply call us on 0113 2435667 to let us know!

Rush 3 day delivery

Left it oh so late? Don't fear we have a 3 working day rush order* service! Choose this at checkout and we will pull out all the stops to get your order produced and out the door in record time. Includes DPD next day trackable delivery.

*Please make sure that when you add Rush 3 day delivery to your order you inform us of your deadline! You can do this in the comment box when you place your order, via email to info@my.hoopscrm.com or simply call us on 0113 2435667 to let us know!
