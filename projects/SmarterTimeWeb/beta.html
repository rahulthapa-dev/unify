Welcome to Smarter Time Beta!

Thank you so much for your interest in Smarter Time - we're glad to have you on board.

We are currently in closed Beta, and we're available on Android only for the time being. If that is you, then you should get your Beta invite in a couple days, as soon as we've processed it!
If you're on another system, then stay tuned for updates as we're working hard to bring Smarter Time to you across the board.

To make sure you receive our email in due time, feel free to add us to your favourite contacts, and make sure you check your Promotions and Spam folders. We send very few emails but we try to make each one of them count!

If you have any questions, you can reach us by hitting the mail envelope icon in the right hand side menu, or write directly to hello @ smartertime.com

We look forward to sharing Smarter Time with you!
