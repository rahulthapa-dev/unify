Course ID: PMTIPM02

Commercial Price: $2395

GSA Price: Please contact gsa@4pmti.com

Unit: per student

Course Description:

The fundamental objective of our 40-hour / 4-day Project Management Professional (PMP)® boot camp is to prepare students for the PMP exam. We provide custom-tailored preparatory material (© Project Management Training Institute) shaped around the nine knowledge areas and five process groups that include over 1000 PMP-style questions.
Our guarantee is simple and straightforward. Your risk is nearly ZERO. We assume all of it. Here is how it works. You enroll in the PMTI PMP® exam prep boot camp and apply for the PMP exam. After going through our training program, you must take the PMP test within 30 days from your training completion date.
If you fail the test on your first attempt, we will:

  • Analyze your results

  • Customize coaching sessions for you (provided through emails and phone calls)

It is your responsibility to take 2nd test within 30 days from the first one.
If you fail on your second attempt, you have to take the 3rd test with in 30 days of the 2nd test date. If you fail on your 3rd attempt, you have 2 options.
Option 1: If you decide not to go through the PMP test again, we will return your training fee.
Option 2: You can attend our training class again for free (Subject to availability).
