PMP Application Audit Process

Why Does PMI Audit PMP Applications?

In order to maintain the integrity of the PMP Certification and enhance the credibility of PMP Certification holders, PMI routinely conducts random audits on a certain percentage of the PMP Exam application.

Although PMI does not publish what percentage gets audited, our 17 years of experience shows about 1 in 4 of the applications get audited.

How Will I Know If I'm Being Audited?

As soon as you submit your application, PMI will notify by email if your application is selected for an audit. They will send over some links to audit documents that you need to complete.

If you havenât received any notification about auditing, then your application is under review. Remember, PMI may still return your application for various reasons including roles and experience that do not qualify as project management related.

The official statement from PMI stated that it will take them 5-7 days to approve the application. However, we have noticed that application verifications that were sent overnight got approved within only a couple of days.
