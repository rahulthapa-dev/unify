Here’s what you’ll get by joining our Atlanta PMP certification training

1. A customized PMP training delivered by our PMP certified instructors

Everyone has their own learning pace. That’s why every instructor in 4PMTI is trained to recognize your strengths and weaknesses and will adapt the PMP training module to suit your individual needs.

Plus, our instructors hold multiple certifications from PMI and other institutions, such as Program Management Professional (PgMP®), Portfolio Management Professional (PfMP®), PMI’s Risk Management Professional (PMI-RMP®), PMI Scheduling Management (PMI-SP®), PMI’s Agile Certified Practitioner (PMI-ACP), Ph.D, CSSMBB, CSM, Certified Disciplined Agile Coach, and more.

2. Hard & digital copies of 4 proprietary PMP Books

PMP Certification Exam Prep Blue Book

The Blue Book will be your main training & guide book during your 4-day PMP training boot camp class.

It’ll provide you with useful examples of each of the 5 process groups and the 10 knowledge areas that’ll be covered in the PMP certification exam using an adult accelerated learning techniques.

PMP Certification Exam Prep Brown Book

This Brown Book is a question bank that has around 1,000 PMP certification test type questions for your practice.

It includes answers, full explanation, reasoning, the knowledge area & process group that corresponds to the questions along with references on where to find the materials related to the subject matter.

PMP Certification Exam Prep Grey Book

The Grey Book is an accompaniment to the Blue Book. It contains inputs, tools, techniques, and outputs (ITTOs) in a workbook format.

You can fill out examples that represent real-life situations and will appear in the PMP certification test in this book.

PMP Certification Exam Prep Orange Book

The Orange Book will provide you with valuable templates that you can use inside the class & for your future projects outside the class.

It’s filled with PMBOK® Guide methodology templates that outlines generally recognized best practices in the industry.

PMBOK® Guide Official Hard-Copy Version (Exam Edition) & Agile Practice Guide

The Guide to the Project Management Body of Knowledge is the most recognized standard for project management in the world, ANSI (American National Standards Institute), and it’s the primary reference of the PMP certification exam.

This book includes a set of guidelines outlined in 49 processes & describes them in detail. It also explains the inputs, tools and techniques, and outputs associated with each of the processes.

Developed by world-renowned experts such as our CEO, Yad Senapathy, this book will explain to you about all the management principles that have been proven successful in companies all over the world.

Additionally, the PMI Agile Practice guide will allow you to ace the PMP exam questions on Agile and Hybrid models of project management.

Complimentary online PMP training courses

Our online PMP training course videos will be available to help you prepare for your PMP certification exam as many times as you like.

You can schedule your PMP exam right after you’ve completed your PMP training, because our 35 contact hours online training course fulfills the requirement set by the PMI (Project Management Institute).

Complete recorded video of PMP training materials

Our in-class PMP training (Led by our Chief Training Officer) will also be recorded & available to you via our online platform.

Supplemental key topic videos

The materials cover topics such as Scope Management to Communications Management and everything in-between, led by our Chief Training Officer. Not only will this strengthen your PMP knowledge even more, it’s jam-packed with practical advice that will help you become a better Project Manager.
