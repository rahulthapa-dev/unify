FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTION

How do I know which service is best for me?
Whether you are concerned about your personal cyber security, or in need of immediate service, we have the right options available for you. Check out our services section to see if any of those matches your situation. Otherwise, please contact us and we will recommend the best option for you.
How do I pay for a service?
It’s easy. You can use different payment options directly from our website. Follow the link under the package you want and choose the payment method that suits you best. We accept debit/ credit card, paypal and mobile banking. And, for One Time Pricing, please contact us before making an order if you have any question.
How will you provide the service?
All of our services are flexible and intuitive. This means it will be customized for your situation. These services can be provided remotely, but we can also send our engineers for field operations.
How do you ensure client data confidentiality?
We enforce strict policies to make sure our clients’ information remains confidential. We sign NDAs and Privacy Policies with all of our clients. We respect and adhere to GDPR compliance.
Are there any additional or hidden charges?
No. We retain complete transparency with our clients about service charges.
How long does it take for you to provide a service?
Usually depends on the service and package. On an average, this takes about 1 to 5 business days.
Do you store client information?
No. We do not store any confidential or sensitive information without clients’ knowledge and consent.
Do you work with law-enforcement?
It depends on the situation and consent of our clients.Yes, we have helped law enforcement to bring criminals to justice in the past.
